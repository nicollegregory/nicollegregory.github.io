“So Latina” is a real playlist that exists on my phone, and as a Latina myself, thought it would only be right to have an entry about it :) Im a huge fan of music, and only limiting my taste to your main stream english pop is so boring. It’s mind boggling to me when an audience has this idea of “oh I can’t understand what they are saying so why would I listen to it”. Lyrics obvious play a huge role, but sound and a beat are just as important. Music is a universal language used to express emotions and experiences. Why let language be a barrier?









POP QUIZ: WHO'S THE ARTIST?







Artist

KALI UCHIS

Year

2024

Kali Uchis is one of my favorite female artists, mainly because of her voice and style. It’s so feminine yet so strong. This song in general is so "girl", a common phrase you here nowadays in this generation lol. Im just a girl trying to make it through life with the help of music. Can you imagine life in silence?? Or can you imagine fi we lived in a world with constant background music. Not sure how that would work, but everyone own personal bubble has background music that only they could hear. Well, I guess that would just be like putting in one AirPod in…. LOLL.

The ‘ORQUIDEAS’ album is a must listen to, highly recommend. The way she was able to blend English and spanish so seamlessly. I love hearing artists include their own culture in their music, whether it be through language or melody, instrumentals, etc. The idea of woman empowerment is a concept I feel strongly about. It is important to recognize the power of women and their potential. Songs with a message, as well as a catchy beat are the ones I tend to emotionally attach myself with.
