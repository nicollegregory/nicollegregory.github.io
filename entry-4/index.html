This weeks theme was space. As someone who loves to create playlists based off a preferred "vibe", space is one of my favorites. Usually during night rides or when I need audio stimulation to tickle my brain. Traveling overseas these past two weeks inspired me to have my space playlist on loop. Especially with flights ranging from 10-20 hours (yikes!!).

song 3: im god.ñ•î ›Å Àñ

Another song that heavily relies on instrumentals. The distorted vocals are almost the siren call vibe, very alluring. Subliminal almost. The voice is unique and airy. The whispers with headphones is the cherry on top.

I feel like blasting music in a car and blasting them in headphones are very different. You physically feel it in a car. The wind in your hair, taking in the scenery with the music ringing in your ears. As a melomaniac, I cannot blast music outload everytime, but I have grown a deep value for my headphones. An item I could not live with out and you will always find on me. Headphones feel a lot more personal.

song 4: like crazy.ñ•î ›Å Àñ

I have a lot to say about this song, but I must stay on track. The lyrics paired with Jimins voice makes the song all more emotional. His voice is so light throughout the song, and then youre struck with this power in the outro. Whispers in the intro and outro pull you into the song. This club-like song, making you want to yell the lyrics at the top of your lungs. The song feels very trendy and makes you want to move.

"Now dont you wake me, I wanna stay in this dream. Dont save me, dont you try to save me. I need a way we, I need a way we can dream on."One of my favorite lyrics to exist. Ive considered it getting it tattooed on me!
