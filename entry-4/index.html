POP QUIZ: WHO'S THE ARTIST?







Artist

THE NEIGHBOURHOOD

Year

2018

The Neighbourhood NEVER misses. I would say the NBHD dominates my space playlist.Their music revolves around themes of relationships and reflection of oneself that can be considered relatable to many listeners. I would describe their content as “heavy”, with lyrics containing dark themes that are sung with so much emotion, hence my attraction to their music. Aside from Jesse Rutherford's insane vocals, the instrumentals hold their own weight, this track specifically. Their music carries this nostalgic, celestial feel to it.

I would almost consider it trippy? (iykyk) Retro and technical influences are common in their tracks. This feature really set off the dream effect that distorts you into a different universe (dramatic but true). Do I sell my house to see them in concert?? Honestly yes. There are a handful of artists that I would drop mad money on and the Neighbourhood is one of them. I need to see more than the typical setlist length though.









POP QUIZ: WHO'S THE ARTIST?









Artist

Mr. Kitty

Year

2014

Cannot remember the first time I found this song, but the feeling it gives me is consistent even years later. I LOVE when music does that. I might not be looping it anymore, but it still hits the way it did on first listen. You ever wish you could listen to a song again for the first time? Though the feeling is similar, it’s not same (insert sad face). I feel that way about shows too. Rewatching shows doesn’t bother me all that much considering my memory is trash and i tend to forget the plot within a year or so.

Back to the song, the piano and rain ambiance almost places you, as a listener, into the song. Now thinking about it, this song in 8D would be fire. A personal concert experience where it feels like the artist is waking around you with a microphone in hand, how cool. It is a bit on the darker side mood wise, but still such an interesting sound. The voice is also unique, deep and techno. 2:20 brings you back from this high that was the chorus and revs you up for the ending of the song. Especially with the voices whispering in my ear??? Out of context I sound insane lol.

POP QUIZ: WHO'S THE ARTIST?





Artist

Bazzi

Year

2018

In general, Bazzi's Cosmic album screams space. On of my favorite albums. Just by looking at the cover art and track titles, you can already sense the vibe. This was an album I frequently listened to in middle school, so I guess I have always been attracted to this kind of sound. Dreams specifically starts of strong with the lyrics "A place, where space and time doesn’t exist...just us". So simple, yet a great intro. There is this run of an instrument throughout the song paired with a bass guitar that I really enjoy. His voice is so… (insert lip bite emoji LMAO).

I was actually struggling to decide which song from this album I wanted to include. Some honorable mentions are ‘Changed’ and ‘Fantasy’, absolute bangers and I recommend. Looking back at it now, this album is decently sexual and I wonder if younger me really registered that upon first listening. If so…thats kinda crazy lol. It weird to think about how our perspectives shift as we get older. Even when it comes to music. I do not remember my initial reactions to this album. I obviously liked it, but now for the same reasons that I have now.
