POP QUIZ: WHO'S THE ARTIST?









Artist

Michael Bublé

Year

2011

MY MAN MY MAN MY MAN (for the season of course). I LOVEEE Michael Bublé. His Christmas album is for sure my most streamed every December. You know it’s kind of a shame that apple replay (similar to Spotify wraps) stop calculating the data after November. Im pretty sure it is because the holidays mess up our streaming stats. But i can sure you the amount of times I am streaming the album is no mistake.

I think I would in fact sell my little sister or house to see this man perform this album during Christmas, sounds life changing. Oh and with hot chocolate??? Honorable mention to Jingle Bells ft The Puppini Sisters, that is my second favorite song on the album. There’s just this specific voice one must have for the Christmas essence. It is also strange to see how fairly “new” Michael Bublé is…I guess? When you compare it to older hits like Brenda Lees ‘Rockin Around the Christmas Tree’ in 1958.
