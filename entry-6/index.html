Artist

CHASE ATLANTIC

Year

2019

CHASE ATLANTIC. That’s its… that’s the entry. Just kidding, but wow I would say they are in my top 3 artists. Their sound is just so cool and my middle school self would have been gagged at the fact that i finally was able to attend one of their concerts. Only took me a 3 hour drive to Philadelphia. Let me tell you… I am NOT a fan of philly. Could not even properly explain it to you but the vibes were off. Aside from that, the concert was PHENOMENAL. Normally I see fangirls cry on the internet and react with a “that crazy” kind of comment. But now that I have experienced that true fangirl shock… i get it. I stood there, with the lights dimmed low and the bass so loud that i felt it below my feet. Staring in awe. HEHE I need to see it again.

I am almost embarrassed by my interest in f boy music. It is so not “feminism” but I can’t help it. The beat goes hard ok? and Christian??? I recorded all of his parts because i could not help myself. They were always my favorite part in the song and seeing it in person felt like an out of body experience (this is me being extra). Something i used to think about a lot was what do normal people do in their free time? This is vague, but hear me out. If you are not fangirling over something, what do you look forward to or what do you look at on social media. These kinda question had me curious about my older sister as i felt like she would never really fangirl over an artist the way i did.

I read about para-social relationships and never thought about it occurring unconsciously. A group of guys i don’t know personally somehow played a huge role in my upbringing, and this event allowed me to reflect on my childhood. Artist

BTS

Year

2015

Kpop is a new tier of boy bands that holds so much negativity against them, it’s annoying. As mentioned in my SO LATINA entry, music holds no barriers and is a universal language. Not so sure why people oppose the idea of trying new things or hate the idea of fun. Nevertheless, BTS holds this boy band energy I longed for after my one direction phase.

This song its off of my favorite album of theirs. “the most beautiful moment of life”. Something that is so attractive about BTS and Kpop in general is that it goes beyond the music. I was quite surprised experiencing a Kpop concert for the first time. I now find American concerts to be a bit underwhelming LOL. Similarly to house parties. Nothing beats a hispanic family party. The primos amongst primos and music playing all night to where the neighbors decide to call the police (true story, i can’t stand my neighbors)!!
