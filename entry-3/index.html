POP QUIZ: WHO'S THE ARTIST?









Artist

Melanie Martinez

Year

2023

Using music as a means to create a story and fantasy is a specialty for Melanie Martinez that I have been obsessed with for years. The way she has created this trilogy following alongside a character she created is what makes her craft so unique. As well as her sound that portrays an “innocent” facade with dark themes. In general, her content is relatable and holds emotional value for me. And the VISUALS that go along are even crazier. She truly is more than just a singers and i appreciate performers like that who clearly show how much they love what they do. Her short film for K-12 was a masterpiece fr. I think artists should do that more, films based of their albums.

Her music reminds me of a grungy fairy, specifically her newest album at this time, portals. This album is a masterpiece and a movie within the tracks. When artists chronologically connect songs within their album, I am always left excited by the concepts, a continuation of this bigger story. It is also just so satisfying to hear one track kind of loop into the next…if that makes sense?Portals is also a tour I did NOT get to go to…. whatever, i don’t even care.

POP QUIZ: WHO'S THE ARTIST?





Artist

Laufey and Beabadoobee

Year

2023

I am actually not a fan of slower songs, because I find it difficult to get hype to, obviously. I only play it i I am doing homework (right now actually) or if I need music to cry to :| Music just holds that much power and can really skew my mood. If done right, I will add a slow song to my playlist, and this is one of those songs done VERY right. Their voices blend together in such a soothing way. With the instrumentals??? The scene need to be just right, walking through Europe or something.

I was very lucky to see Laufey in concert, even though I wasn’t well versed in her music. I can assure you though I became a fan when i walked out of the venue. Seeing an artist live is SO different in comparison to just listening to their songs on your phone. The voice carries itself, but hearing it live was so different. That’s what I love about concerts. The talent really slaps you in the face and has you question why you weren’t blessed with the musically inclined talent gene, awk :0
